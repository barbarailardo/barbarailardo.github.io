Welcome to ADHDologia!

Bridging the gap between personal experience and neuroscience to help inspire change and growth.

Embracing neurodiversity

Hi! I'm Barbara Sofia Ilardo, but you can call me Barb.

I created ADHDologia to empower and support adults navigating neurodivergence, particularly ADHD and autism, by bridging the gap between personal experience and the latest scientific knowledge.

My community and I envision a society where neurodiversity is not only understood but celebrated and empowered, allowing neurodivergent folks to embrace their strengths, overcome challenges, and thrive in all areas of life including their career.

About me

I was born in Argentina and spent a somewhat nomad and problematic childhood and adolescence between Los Angeles, Spain and Italy.

Even though my school grades were high, my self-esteeem and emotional regulation were quite the opposite. I'm your typical chronically-burned-out overachiever (though I'm working on it!).

During my PhD in Population Genomics at John Moores Liverpool University, I discovered my neurodivergence thanks to social media and a subsequent clinical evaluation.

ADHDologia is shaped by many things, including my personal experiences as an ADHD and autistic person, my academic background in Population Genomics and Educational Psychology, my experience in Graphic Design and Full Stack software development and, last but not least, the invaluable feedback and engagement of the #neurospicy community on the @ADHDologia socials.
